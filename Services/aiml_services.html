Unleash the Power of our PCB Error Detection solution!

PCB error Detection

Our PCB defect detection solution, leveraging two primary techniques: image subtraction and feature extraction. By comparing the PCB under inspection with a reference image, defects are revealed through the subtracted image. For image augmentation, we employ the powerful combination of normalization, Laplacian, and Sobel methods.
